About the Band

The Clarks were formed in 1986 while attending Indiana University of Pennsylvania and have coined the genre "Pennsylvania Rock"
thanks to their surprising blend of rock, alternative, and country. 34 years and elevn stuio albums later, The Clarks continue to tour
and record and even play public Facebook Live shows to cater to their beloved fan-base.
